Dermatology

Our dermatology department embraces both clinical and cosmetic dermatology services. It is specialized in the diagnosis and management of various skin conditions and provides solutions to various cosmetic problems. Some of the common conditions like atopic dermatitis, eczemas, psoriasis, lichen planus, seborrheic dermatitis are delt with specialized care and expertise. At the same time cosmetic problems like acne, melasma, hair loss, male and female pattern hair loss etc. are managed both medically and with advanced procedures like micro needling, chemical peels, PRP etc.

Treatments and Procedures offered are:

  • Treatment for various eczemas
  • Pediatric dermatology
  • Psoriasis and lichen planus treatments
  • Alopecia and keloid intra lesional injections
  • Acne and scar management
  • Hair loss evaluation and treatment- PRP hair
  • Pigmentations, acne scars and melasma - chemical peels
  • Skin rejuvenation, fine wrinkles and scars - micro needling
  • Skin tags and warts - electrocautery
  • Molluscum- chemical cautery
  • Thermoscopic evaluation of skin hair and nails
